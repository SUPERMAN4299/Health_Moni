How to Login

Important: You will receive a card in your product box containing your Username and Password. Enter them exactly as shown in the app to access our service.

Follow these steps carefully:

  1. Open the box where you received this product.
  2. On your desktop, download the software from our website: Download Here
  3. Install the software on your PC. Make sure Python is installed: Download Python
  4. Before opening the app, ensure your PC is connected to the Wi-Fi network associated with the device.
  5. Open the software and enter the Username and Password provided on your card.
  6. Connect the device over Wi-Fi through the software.
  7. Allow the software to access the device.
  8. Once connected, you will be redirected to the monitoring dashboard with real-time graphs.
